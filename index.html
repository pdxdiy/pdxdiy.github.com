PDX DIY is...

PDX DIY is part of a global community of DIY clubs. Also, DIY is a community where tens of thousands of kids get to share what they do and learn skills.

What we do

PDX DIY club is a group that works together to practice skills (like Bike Mechanic), do creative projects (like make a game from scrap), and take epic adventures together (like exploring the Japanese Gardens while checking off botanist skills). We get together periodially and are just a bunch of friends hacking on random projects.

How to join

Step 1:

Sign up at http://diy.org.

Step 2:

Email your diy.org nickname to ben@pdxdiy.org

Volunteer

We're currently seeking volunteers to help with our outreach efforts. Increasingly, we are involved in Maker Faires, museum programs, and other exciting events around the Portland Area. We're looking for folks interested in education and capable at building things to help us as we develop this program. Some volunteer tasks would include: sourcing materials, hands-on support at workshops, building life-size versions of this - just to name a few.
