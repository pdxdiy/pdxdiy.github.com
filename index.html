Calling All Young Bike Mechanics

You are invited to a Bike Mechanic challenge at Santiam Bicycle. Learn some great bike mechanic skills like how to fix a flat and how to replace your bike grips. Also trick out your bike with us. Do all 3 activities to get your bike mechanic patch on diy.org!

Details:

We will be completing various challenges outlined here - https://diy.org/skills/bikemechanic. Bringing your bike is encouraged. Complete 3 challenges and get a virtual bike mechanic badge and a PDX DIY sticker!

Time: Saturday, June 22nd 2-4pm

Cost: Free

Age range: 16 and under

How to sign up: First, go sign up for diy.org - https://diy.org/ (do this for each person that will be participating). Then, send your diy.org nickname(s) to ben@pdxdiy.org. That's it!

Questions? Contact ben@pdxdiy.org

About PDX DIY

PDX DIY is...

PDX DIY is part of a global community of DIY clubs. Also, DIY is a community where tens of thousands of kids get to share what they do and learn skills.

What we do

PDX DIY club is a group that works together to practice skills (like Bike Mechanic), do creative projects (like make a game from scrap), and take epic adventures together (like exploring the Japanese Gardens while checking off botanist skills). We get together periodially and are just a bunch of friends hacking on random projects.

How to join

Step 1:

Sign up at http://diy.org.

Step 2:

Email your diy.org nickname to ben@pdxdiy.org
