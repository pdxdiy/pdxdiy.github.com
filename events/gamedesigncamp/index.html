Questions and Answers

When?

Camp is June 22-24 from 3-6pm.

What is the ideal camper age?

Ages 8-16 are ideal for design club members. If you are not in this age range, it is still possible to join design club, but we want to talk to you first.

What will I need to bring?

We will provide the parts and tools for assembly, you'll want to bring a laptop for to work on graphics (Chromebooks work great).

Will I get to keep my game?

Absolutely! Not only will you be able to keep your game, but you'll also get to keep all the awesome designer skills you'll learn!
