Rose City Make 'n Take 2014

Making / Learning / Tinkering / Having Fun

The Maker culture is spreading across the nation, and Portland is no exception. The second annual Rose City Make N’ Take is a day for the “makers” of Portland, both young and old to spend the day together exploring, experimenting and creating. Don’t feel like you have to be an expert at something to come, this is the perfect place for everyone to learn and play.

Makers from all over the Portland community will gather together to offer unique experiences. Get out of the house and work with others to “make” everything from robots to LED wearables and many things in between. Whether you are an artist, an engineer, an inventor, a teacher or a student - there’s something for everyone. In addition to exploring multiple booths in the cafeteria, there will be a movie screening and coding sessions in the computer lab.

If you haven’t tried a Maker Faire yet, this is a perfect way to spend your Saturday. If you have attended a Maker Faire, even more reason to come and lend your experience to the fun and help spread the joy. Once you attend a Maker Faire, you’ll never want to miss one again.
