Questions and Answers

When?

NodeBot camp is July 27-29 from 3-6pm.

What is the ideal camper age?

Ages 10-16 are ideal for campers. If you are not in this age range, it is still possible to join us, but we want to talk to you first.

What will I need to bring?

We will provide the parts and tools for assembly, you'll need to bring a laptop for programming (Chromebooks work great).

Will I get to keep my robot?

Absolutely! Not only will you be able to keep your robot, but you'll also get to keep all the awesome hacker skills you'll be learning at camp!
