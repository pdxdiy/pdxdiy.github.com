Soldering Workshop

All Ages

Come hang out and learn to solder. We'll start with a soldering clinic and then a lab where you'll make your own electronic jar of fireflies. We will provide the tools and have starter kits available for purchase.

When: Sunday (8/21) 1-3pm

Where: Craft Lab (7608 N Interstate Ave. Portland, OR 97217)

Agenda:

(1-2 pm) Basics of Soldering Clinic

This 1 hour session will be an instructor led step-by-step clinic on how to solder. It is great for getting started or picking up some tricks of the soldering trade.

(2-3 pm) Soldering Lab

This 1 hour lab will be time to work on your project and get help from others in practicing your solder craft.

Space is limited, so RSVP soon to get your spot.

This is an all ages workshop. Parent required for kids under 13. Parent Participation is encouraged! Work as a pair or get a get of your own. We'll have plenty of spots for parents.
