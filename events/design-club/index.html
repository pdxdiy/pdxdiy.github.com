DESIGN CLUB

Design is an ancient human discipline. Let's practice it.

Learn

Learn different aspects of design through discussion and hands on projects. Also, Get to hear stories from local designers and be a part of Q&A sessions.

Practice

Create design projects of your own. Work or different disciplines of design or focus on one that suites your design fancy.

Tool Up

With PDX DIY's Tool Lending Library, get access tools like silk screen kits, a sewing machine, or a 3d printer.

Be Awesome

Get a DIY.org Extra Awesome Membership, to document and start a portfolio. Plus get a bunch of additional benefits, like free patches.

What is the ideal age?

Ages 11-16 are ideal for design club members. If you are not in this age range, it is still possible to join design club, but we want to talk to you first.

Will you provide project materials?

We will have some materials available, but you are responsible for the materials of your projects. Besides it is really fun to be thrifty and find deals on really cool stuff!
