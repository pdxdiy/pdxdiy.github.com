Getting Started

Step 1: Get a DIY Account

We will use DIY.org for capturing and documenting progress in your portfolio. If you don't already have an account, sign up for one. Parents - get involved too and join as a parent on DIY.

*NOTE: I am working through the details of payment with DIY for the Extra Awesome Memberships. You will hear from me by October 1st about more how to get this.

Join DIY.org

Step 2: Tell us about yourself

This is general contact info, your diy nickname, and your design interests.

Tell us about yourself

Step 3: Check out the Tool Lending Library

We have a bunch of resources that you can borrow. Take a look and see what kind of resources we have.

Check out Tool Lending Library

Step 4: Add something to your Portfolio!

Get some portfolio practice by uploading a pic or video to your DIY portfolio. Extra points if it is related to design.

Questions and Answers

When do you meet?

Starting October 9th, we'll be meeting every 2nd and 4th Thursday from 6-8pm. We will just be doing this for the fall season, so we'll have our last meetup on December 11th.

What is the ideal age?

Ages 11-16 are ideal for design club members. If you are not in this age range, it is still possible to join design club, but we want to talk to you first.

Will you provide project materials?

We will have some materials available, but you are responsible for the materials of your projects. Besides it is really fun to be thrifty and find deals on really cool stuff!

How long does my DIY Extra Awesome membership last?

Your extra awesome membership will last through the end of 2014. Did you see that you get unlimited free embroidered patches?!?
