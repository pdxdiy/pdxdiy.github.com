Step 3: Check out the Tool Lending Library

Borrow rather than buy

We have a bunch of resources that you can borrow. From artist pen sets to 3d printers, if you are looking for something that will help your design project, take a look and see what kind of resources we have.

Check out Tool Lending Library

Step 4: Assemble Your Toolkit

Having the essentials is key

There are some things that you'll want to bring to each club meeting. Here is a checklist of them:


  • Bring something to take pictures with. An iPad, iPod, or a simple camera works.

  • Bring something to doodle with or take notes. A journal or notepad and a pen or pencil. I love spiral bound, but that is up to you.

  • Bring something that inspires your inner designer. This can come in a number of forms. Be it an awesome graphic designer magazine, a list of game designs methods, or just a favorite design pic. Have something that inspires you.

  • Also, Laptops are great to have, but are totally optional.
