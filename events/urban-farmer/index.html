Companion Plant Workshop

All Ages

Companion planting by definition is simply any plant that is purposefully planted next to another to enhance growth, beauty or flavor. Typically we see companion plants that do any number of supportive things for both vegetable gardens and our ornamental plants.

In this group learning environment you will be planting a selection of vegetables while also learning why these plants will thrive together. These custom planted containers will contain a small symbiotic farm piece for your home production of fresh food! Conserving space and utilizing so much all in one medium sized plasctic pot.

You will also have open forum to ask us anything you like on this topic. Your specific growing goals are all different. Do you want to attract beneficial insects to your veggie garden?
